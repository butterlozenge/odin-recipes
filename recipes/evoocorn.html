Amy's Famous EVOO-corn

EVOO corn

Yes, this is popcorn. But not just any popcorn; it's EVOO corn. This small change makes a huge difference in flavor. I encourage you to give it a try over your normal buttered popcorn.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp of oil
  • 1/2 cup of popcorn kernels
  • 2 tbsp of the best EVOO you can find

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a tall pot (the kernels will fly)
  2. Once oil is up to temp, add enough kernels to cover the bottom of the pot
  3. Keep lid partially on while kernels pop
  4. Shake every so often to ensure popped kernels won't stick to the bottom of the pot and burn
  5. Transfer popcorn to a large bowl and drizzle with EVOO
  6. Enjoy!
